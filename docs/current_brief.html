💡 Market Opportunity
Why This Topic Now

The ocean theme is currently popular among parents of 2-4 year olds, making this niche a good opportunity for a digital product creator.

What To Build

The Ocean Explorer Busy Book is a 25-page PDF activity pack containing colouring pages, mazes, and ocean-themed puzzles. It has interactive elements such as 'spot the fish' and 'count the seashells' to keep children engaged. The pack also includes a certificate of completion to reward kids for finishing the activities.

Why It Sells
  • Parents of 2-4 year olds preparing for preschool or kindergarten buy it to keep their children entertained and learning during summer breaks
  • The search trigger is year-round, but peaks in June and July as parents seek screen-free activities for their kids
  • The emotional reason parents buy it is to provide a structured, educational, and fun experience for their children while fostering a love for the ocean
🔍 Competitor Analysis What's already selling on Etsy
Little Explorer Printables

Your Differentiators
  • A 'day and night' ocean scene that children can colour and compare
  • A set of reusable stickers featuring different ocean creatures
  • A page with fun facts about marine life, written in simple language for young children
🎯 Market Gaps What competitors are missing
Competitors often lack a comprehensive guide for parents on how to use the busy book effectively
There is an opportunity to create a busy book with more complex activities for older children in the 2-4 age range
