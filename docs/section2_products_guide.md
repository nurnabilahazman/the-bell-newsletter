# Section 2 — Digital Products Guide
## How to Create, List, and Sell Your 3 Weekly Products

---

### The Process (Every Week)
1. Read the product suggestion in the newsletter
2. Look at the store inspiration link — study their best-selling listing
3. Paste the creation prompt into Claude
4. Claude generates the full content — copy it into Canva or Google Sheets
5. Export as PDF or share the Sheet
6. List it on your store (once your store is ready)
7. Tick ✓ Products Created in your Google Sheet

---

### Tool 1: Canva (for PDF printables)
Free at **canva.com**. This is where you design language workbooks, children's activity pages, and productivity printables.

**Setting up for printables:**
- Create account at canva.com (free)
- Click **Create a design** → **Custom size** → set to **8.5 x 11 in** (US Letter, standard for printables)
- Use **A4 (210 x 297mm)** if targeting UK/Australia/Asia buyers

**Quick design workflow:**
1. Paste Claude's content into a Canva template
2. Use brand colours: Navy `#1A1A2E`, Gold `#C9A84C`, White `#FFFFFF`
3. Font for headings: **Playfair Display** or **Cormorant Garamond**
4. Font for body: **Lato** or **Montserrat**
5. Export: **File → Download → PDF Print** (highest quality)

**Free Canva templates to start from:**
- Search "worksheet" in Canva templates
- Search "planner" for productivity products
- Search "flashcard" for language products
- Search "activity page" for children's products

---

### Tool 2: Google Sheets (for tracker/spreadsheet products)
Free. Buyers get a copy of your sheet they can use immediately.

**How to share a Google Sheets product:**
1. Build the sheet with Claude's prompt
2. File → Share → change to "Anyone with the link can view"
3. File → Download → Microsoft Excel (.xlsx) — sell the Excel file
4. Or: sell a "template" link that forces the buyer to make their own copy

**Protecting your work:**
- Before selling, go to **Data → Protect Sheets and Ranges**
- Lock the formula cells so buyers can't accidentally break it
- Leave the input cells (where they type their data) unlocked

### Where to Sell

**Getting started (before your store is ready):**
- Build the products now, store them in a Google Drive folder
- You need ~10 products before opening a store looks credible

**Platforms to sell on:**
| Platform | Best for | Fee |
|---|---|---|
| **Etsy** | Printables, templates, worksheets | $0.20/listing + 6.5% transaction |
| **Gumroad** | Digital downloads, bundles | 10% on free plan, $10/mo for lower fees |
| **Payhip** | Simple digital products | Free (5% fee) or $29/mo (no fee) |
| **Teachers Pay Teachers** | Educational/children's content | Free to join, 45-80% royalty |

**Start with Gumroad** — zero setup cost, instant payouts, simplest interface.

---

### Pricing Strategy

| Product type | Suggested price |
|---|---|
| Single printable (1-2 pages) | $2 – $4 |
| Worksheet pack (5-10 pages) | $5 – $9 |
| Full workbook (20+ pages) | $10 – $18 |
| Google Sheets tracker | $5 – $15 |
| Bundle (3+ products) | $12 – $25 |

**Never price below $2.** Cheap products get fewer sales, not more — buyers assume low quality.

---

### What Makes a Product Sell

**Listings with mockup images sell 3–5x more than listings with just the PDF preview.**
- Use Canva's mockup feature: search "iPad mockup" or "notebook mockup" in Canva
- Show the product filled in with sample data, not blank

**Title formula that works:**
`[Specific adjective] [Product type] | [Who it's for] | [Key benefit]`
Example: *Minimalist Weekly Budget Tracker | Google Sheets | Paycheck-to-Paycheck Planning*

**Must-have listing elements:**
- At least 5 photos/mockups
- Description that answers: what is it, what's included, who is it for, how do they use it
- Clear file format info (PDF, A4/US Letter, Google Sheets link, etc.)
- "Instant download" in the title if it's a digital product

---

### Quality Checklist Before Listing

- [ ] Spell-checked (use Grammarly — free)
- [ ] All formulas tested with sample data (for spreadsheets)
- [ ] PDF exported at print quality (300 DPI)
- [ ] At least one mockup image created
- [ ] Priced correctly for the category
- [ ] Title includes the main keyword buyers search for
