💡 The Pricing Ladder Strategy
Start at $3.99–$4.99 to earn your first 10 reviews fast. Productivity buyers have higher price tolerance than children's printable buyers — once you have 25 reviews and a polished thumbnail, $6.99–$7.99 is fully justified. OpulentPlanners charges $9.99+ simply because of design quality. Your design is just as good.
🎯 Market Gaps — Build These First Low competition, proven demand, no clear winner yet
🎯 Undated Planners
Dated planners die every December. Undated ones sell year-round forever. Every planner you make should be undated.
💼 Profession-Specific Trackers
Teacher income trackers, freelancer expense logs, Etsy seller planners. Low competition, premium price tolerance.
🌙 Evening Routine + Wind-Down Kits
Morning routines are saturated. Evening routines are not. HeyMorning owns mornings — evenings are wide open.
📊 Data Dashboard Aesthetic
GridEx proves there's a buyer for dashboard-style trackers. Minimal, grid-heavy, data-forward design. Almost no direct competitors.
🧠 Deep Work + Focus Sheets
Time-blocking, deep work sessions, Pomodoro logs. PrioriDigitalStudio is the only clear player. Knowledge workers pay more.
🌿 Wellness × Productivity Hybrid
Gratitude + goals + self-care in one planner. SerenatasJourney proves this crossover sells. Much less saturated than pure productivity.

Your Differentiation

  • Weekly overview page (Mon–Sun + weekly goal)

  • Font: Montserrat headings + Lato body
  • Colors: Cream background, charcoal text, gold or teal accent
  • Sell as 90-day (lower price) AND 365-day (higher price) versions
  • Export: PDF Print

Goal Setting Workbook (Quarterly Edition)
Price: $5.99 launch → $7.99 Build time: 3–4 hrs Canva Pages: 35+ Format: PDF Workbook Demand: High
Your Differentiation
Workbooks command 2× the price of single trackers because they feel complete and intentional. Quarterly (90-day) beats annual — it's a lower commitment ask and buyers return every quarter. PrioriDigitalStudio and OpulentPlanners own this space at the premium end. Your edge: include a 'Wheel of Life' assessment + weekly check-in pages — most workbooks skip these, but they're what buyers mention wanting in reviews.
What to include
  • Wheel of Life assessment (8 life areas scored 1-10)
  • Values clarification exercise
  • 3 big goals for the quarter
  • Monthly milestones for each goal
  • Weekly check-in pages (12 weeks)
  • Quarterly review + reflection
  • Affirmations page
  • Certificate/closing
Design specs
  • Premium design — dark navy or deep charcoal + gold accent
  • Font: Playfair Display headings + Lato body
  • A4 or Letter portrait
  • 35+ pages minimum
  • Export: PDF Print
  • Price at $5.99 launch — this earns reviews faster than starting too high
