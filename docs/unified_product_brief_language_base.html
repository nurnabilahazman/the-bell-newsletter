💡 Script Languages Command Higher Prices
Japanese, Korean, Arabic, and Chinese products consistently sell at $1–$2 more than equivalent Latin-alphabet products. Buyers know these materials require more expertise to produce — font selection, stroke order, character accuracy. This perceived difficulty = price premium you can capture from day one.

Your Differentiation
MusesofBlanc does aesthetic French products but grammar cheat sheets are almost empty on Etsy.   • Example sentences in a table (not bullet list)

Travel Phrasebook — 5 Languages
Price: $5.99 launch → $7.99 Build time: 3–4 hrs Canva Pages: 45+ Format: PDF Phrasebook + Wallet Cards Demand: Medium + Seasonal
Your Differentiation
No dominant player owns the multi-language travel phrasebook on Etsy. Your edge: sell it with a separate 'wallet card' file — one folded A6 card per language with the top 20 phrases. Buyers love having a physical card in their passport. Include a 'Pronunciation Cheat Code' page per language using phonetic English approximations (not IPA) — travelers don't know IPA but they understand 'say: bon-ZHOOR'.
What to include
  • 5 language sections (Spanish, French, Italian, German, Portuguese)
  • Each: Greetings, Numbers 1–20, Directions, Restaurant, Shopping, Emergencies, Hotel — 10 phrases per topic per language
  • Pronunciation in plain phonetic English after each phrase
  • Wallet card file: top 20 phrases per language, folds to A6/business card size
  • Total: 45+ pages + 5 wallet card files
Design specs
  • Compact A5 or half-letter portrait
  • Font: Clean sans-serif throughout
  • Color: Flag-color accent per language section (subtle — not gaudy)
  • Phonetic pronunciation in italics + muted color
  • Wallet card: A4 sheet folded to A6 — include fold guide
  • Export: Main PDF + separate wallet cards file
